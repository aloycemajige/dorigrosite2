Our Vision

Our vision is to become an industrial leader in the production and supply of high quality food , working together with small holder farmers to meet the need of worldwide consumers and other relevant stakeholders in our value chain.

Mission

To eliminate food waste in Africa with efficient food distribution systems while ensuring smallholder farmers have a stable and growing source of income.

About Us

Dorigro foods is one of the players in the food production and processing industry in Tanzania . We are engaged in production and sale of variety food crops in vegetable and grain. We deliver wide range of fresh produce directly from farms to stores of B2B customers-retailers, wholesalers, local vendors, restaurants, hotels. Dorigro foods also offer Technical services to its smallholder farmers, and ensures the products/services offered the customer/farmer is highly beneficial to their needs. Our current business units include:

  • Agricultural production
  • Poultry farming & Hatchery Services
